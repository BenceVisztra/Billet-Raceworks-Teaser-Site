A new category of electric racing machines is right around the corner. Billet Raceworks is developing a high‑performance electric raceboard engineered around real racing tires from karting, custom precision billet aluminium components, and motorsport‑grade engineering. Built for extreme cornering, stability, and best in class power.

Chassis specs:
- Karting racing tires 10x4.5-5
- Custom designed billet aluminium chassis
- Truck angle altering swingarms, which increase turning when loaded by cornering forces
- Tito Duality trucks with Riptide bushings
- All electronics fit inside the chassis - no ugly top mounted electronics

"Top of the line" electronics setup (ONLY FOR PROFESSIONALS)
- 30-50kW peak power (if you have the balls for it) - depending on battery configuration - calculated from battery amps, NOT phase amps
- 20S5P / 20S6P tabless cell battery config, or fits 10x 4S 9.5Ah Lipos for a 20S config
- G300 / 2G300 VESCs from MakerX
- 4x 56154 inrunners from SSS motors - 280 / 330 KV options
- Custom double stage gear drives (coming few months after the chassis)

"Reasonable" electronics setup (still enough to race pro category for most tracks)
- 20S5P / 20S6P tabless cell battery pack
- 4x Reacher motors - recommended config 6385 front, 6395 rear
- D100s / SKP Solo VESCs

Developed by Bence Visztra — professional racer and engineer with frequent race podiums validating the board’s design philosophy.
